1) Apps Covered

This Policy primarily covers Huni (viewer app). If you also use Huni Studio (creator/seller app), the same principles apply: we collect only what is needed to operate the features you choose to use. Some data categories described below apply only when a feature is available and enabled.

2) Information We Collect

2.1 Information you provide

  • Support requests. If you contact us, we receive the information you provide (e.g., email, message content, screenshots).
  • Reports & safety messages. If you use in-app Report features, we may collect your selected reason and any optional message you submit, plus identifiers needed to find the reported item (e.g., content/channel ID) to review abuse and safety issues.
  • Account information (if you choose to sign in). Huni can be used without signing in. If you choose to create an account or sign in (where available), we may receive identifiers from the authentication provider (such as email address or a provider ID) and basic profile fields you choose to set (such as display name).
  • Orders / shopping requests (if available). Some versions of the Service may allow you to submit an order request or contact a seller. In that case, we collect the details you enter (e.g., recipient name, phone number, shipping address, notes, and items) and deliver them to the seller/creator account so they can fulfill your request.

2.2 On-device data (stored locally)

To personalize your experience and improve performance, Huni may store certain data on your device (e.g., using local storage/cache), such as:

  • Followed channels/shops, blocked channels, and related preferences
  • Watch history and playback settings (e.g., volume/mute, player configuration)
  • Cached images/thumbnails and other temporary performance files

You can typically clear on-device data by uninstalling the app or clearing the app’s storage/cache in your device settings.

2.3 Push notifications (optional)

If you enable notifications, we may collect and store a push token (e.g., an Expo/APNs token) and basic delivery metadata (such as timestamps and delivery status) to send you notifications (for example, new content updates or important service messages).

  • Notification preferences. Your opt-in/opt-out status and preference settings may be stored to respect your choices.
  • Identity linking (guest vs. signed-in). To avoid duplicate notifications and to deliver notifications correctly, the Service may associate a push token with a guest identifier on your device and/or with your user account if you sign in.

2.4 Subscriptions / In-App Purchases (Huni Premium)

If you purchase Huni Premium via Apple In-App Purchase, payments are processed by Apple. We do not receive your full payment card details. We may receive information necessary to provide Premium access, such as:

  • Subscription status/entitlement (active/expired)
  • Product identifier (e.g., monthly subscription), transaction timestamps, and restore results
  • Receipt/transaction verification signals (where applicable)

2.5 Technical and usage data

Like most online services, our infrastructure and service providers may process standard technical data to operate and secure the Service:

  • IP address, device type/model, OS version, app version, language/region
  • Crash reports and basic diagnostic logs (for debugging and stability)
  • Network and performance metrics (e.g., load times, errors)

We use this information for service operation, security, debugging, and reliability—not to try to personally identify you.
